What are Strictly Necessary Cookies?

Strictly necessary cookies, are data files put on your device (computer and/or mobile) directly by the site or app you are using and the data file(s) are NOT sent to another site or app (third party). This is also known as a first-party cookie and it is not required to obtain consent for this type of cookie.
